About the game:

See FPS featured in Northeastern University News!

As the newcomer to the weirdly unique, but conflict-ridden tribes of the now overgrown "Toni Areal", the hero of the game is tasked with bringing peace to these strange people by uniting them in the face of a common threat.

Solve puzzles and the tribespeople's problems by stuffing them into your bag, and getting them where they need to go!​​

My work for the game:

My primary role for this game is that of level designer/ 3D artist. As lead artist, I collaborate with other team memebers to produce 2D art assets and marketing materials.
As a team, we all work together on the general game design to figure out the best solutions for vital aspects such as characters, gameplay and controls, and so on!
Smaller additional tasks I complete regularly include assisting with online project management.
