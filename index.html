About Me

3D artist, world builder, level designer

Hey there!

Hi! My name is Anna, and I'm a senior at Northeastern University majoring in Game Design. I transferred to Northeastern in fall 2017, after completing my first year at the Zurich University of the Arts in my home country, Switzerland.

From May to August in 2018 I had the pleasure to work as a level builder on Lantana Games' Children of Liberty and Mondrian: Plastic Reality.

Currently a lot of my energy goes into the senior project I'm working on with four outstanding people, Fruit Postal Service! For this top-down party racing game I produce any and all 3D assets, co-design and build the levels, and collaborate with the team in defining gameplay aspects. At the MassDigi Game Challenge 2019, Fruit Postal Service was awarded the title of Category Winner, and now we're excited to be headed to PAX East and other upcoming gaming events!
