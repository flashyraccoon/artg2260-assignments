The Hermit's Cottage

My work for the model:

I fully modelled this quaint little cottage from scratch using Cinema 4D. The textures for the model came, for the most part, from Alun David Bestor. In addition to these textures I also created some custom materials in Cinema 4D, to be used for the weather vane on the rooftop, as well as for the lantern hanging by the door. The surrounding grassy area was created in Cinema 4D as well, and for the skybox I used a free-to-use image of a forest.

Tools I used:

  • Cinema 4D
