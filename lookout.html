About the mod:

The Lookout is a player home for any kind of Skyrim-player! whether you're an RPG enthusiast, immersing yourself in your very own story, or just looking for a place to store your in-game riches; this home will provide a cozy retreat on the inside, and breathtaking views on the outside.
